About Diwali

Diwali, also known as Deepavali, is the Hindu festival of lights celebrated every year. The festival spiritually signifies the victory of light over darkness, good over evil, knowledge over ignorance, and hope over despair.

Traditions & Customs

Lighting Diyas

Decorating homes with oil lamps to welcome Goddess Lakshmi

Rangoli

Creating colorful floor designs using colored powder

Prayers

Performing Lakshmi Puja for prosperity and wealth

Sweets

Sharing traditional sweets and gifts with loved ones

Diwali Gallery

How to Celebrate

Diwali is celebrated over five days, with each day having its own significance and rituals. The celebration includes cleaning and decorating homes, lighting diyas, creating rangoli, exchanging gifts, and enjoying festive meals with family and friends.
